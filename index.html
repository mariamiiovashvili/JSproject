OUR STORY

We spent the last few years cultivating a blend of crafted gourmet organic coffee. That will bring light into your hearts with every sip. We strive to put a extra dose of happiness into your cup daily.

We are not only rising for people. We are rising for our planet. Each bag of coffee sold goes towards planting a tree across the world. To help offset carbon emissions, and take action against climate change.

Locally based in Warszawa, Poland

Local deliveries and services in Warszawa, Poland

Whole Sale Coffee shop supplier.

Our Coffee Farms

We source the beans from many tiny family farms.

Some at high elevation with shade grown coffee fields that flourish on the edge of the mountains and some at medium elevation with full sun.

The workers hand pick every coffee cherry, and hand wash them too. Matter of fact, they do it all by hand right down to the drying of the beans in natural sunlight with beans spread over wooden planks.

The same way it was done hundreds of years ago, an old world method. No automated picking machines or careless processing. Hand selected beans, among the best anywhere in the world.

We are sure you can not get coffee of this quality for the price we offer it anywhere else. We focus on environmentally friendly processing that is good for the surrounding habitat.
